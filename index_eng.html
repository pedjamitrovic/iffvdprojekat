Welcome!

Welcome to the website of the International Film Festival, which will be held from June 24 to July 5 in Belgrade.

The first IFF, under the title "Brave New World", was opened on January 9, 1971 in the Dom Sindikata, with projection of the movie "Mesh" by Robert Altman. If you are interested, you can read more about the history of the Festival here.

This year, the Festival will be held from June 24th to July 5th and will be shown several exceptional movies by foreign directors.

During its existence, the IFF featured movies starring movie stars like Jack Nicholson, Kirk Douglas, Robert De Niro, and who are movie stars this year see here.

The venue is Belgrade, a beautiful city at the confluence of the Danube and Sava rivers, which hosts many cultural events, including the IFF. Films will be shown in several cinemas, in front of more than 100000 viewers.

You can book the tickets on the page of the desired movie or buy it in one of our own marketplace.

Festival participants will be awarded in six categories. In the charge of evaluation will be our expert jury composed of famous names of Serbian cinematography.

In the news section we will anounce all important information regarding the Festival.
